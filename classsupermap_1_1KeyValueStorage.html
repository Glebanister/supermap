template<typename Key, typename Value, typename IndexT>

An abstract key-value storage.

Template Parameters
Keytype of key.
Valuetype of value.
IndexTtype of storage size. Could be non-integral, if, for example, storage size is not easily calculable, then Bounds could be a replacement.
